About the Game

A New Home Among the Stars

In Distant Colony, humanity’s fate rests in your hands. As commander of a deep-space expedition, you lead a team of pioneers to an uncharted, resource-rich planet—humanity’s last hope for survival. With limited supplies and no way back, you must establish a thriving colony from the ground up.

Survival is not guaranteed. Manage scarce resources, harness alien technology, and adapt to extreme environmental conditions. The planet’s atmosphere is volatile, its terrain unpredictable, and its native lifeforms—unfriendly. Will you withstand the unknown, or succumb to the forces of nature?

Build, Expand, and Protect

  • Construct modular bases, research advanced technology, and develop a self-sustaining infrastructure.
  • Mine rare minerals, cultivate food, and produce energy to keep your colony alive.
  • Expand your influence beyond your settlement—explore ruins, uncover lost knowledge, and forge alliances (or conflicts) with alien civilizations.

Shape the Future of Your Colony

Your decisions determine the fate of your settlers. Will you create a utopia or a ruthless dictatorship? Will your colony embrace diplomacy, or will it conquer its neighbors?

The stars are waiting. But survival is only the beginning.
